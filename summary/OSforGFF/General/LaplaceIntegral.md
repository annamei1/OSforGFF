## Overview

This file proves the Laplace integral identity (a special case of the modified Bessel function $K_{1/2}$):
$$\int_0^\infty s^{-1/2} e^{-a/s - bs}\, ds = \sqrt{\pi/b}\, e^{-2\sqrt{ab}},$$
valid for $a \geq 0$, $b > 0$. The proof proceeds in six stages: (1) substituting $s = t^2$ to
clear the half-power; (2) completing the square in the exponent; (3) factoring out
$e^{-2\sqrt{ab}}$; (4) rescaling by $u = \sqrt{b}\,t$; (5) reducing to the Glasser integral
$\int_0^\infty e^{-(c/u - u)^2}\, du = \sqrt{\pi}/2$ via the Cauchy-Schlömilch identity; and
(6) combining all steps.
