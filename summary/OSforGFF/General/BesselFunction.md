## Overview

This file defines the modified Bessel function $K_1(z)$ via its cosh integral representation
$K_1(z) = \int_0^\infty e^{-z\cosh t}\cosh t\,dt$ and establishes the key analytic properties
needed for the free covariance in Euclidean AQFT. The main results are: positivity and
continuity of $K_1$ on $(0,\infty)$; exponential decay $K_1(z) \leq (\sinh 1 + 2)e^{-z}$ for
$z \geq 1$; near-origin bounds $K_1(z) \leq (\cosh 1 + 2)/z$ for $z \in (0,1]$; integrability
of the radial kernel $r^2 K_1(mr)$ on $(0,\infty)$; and the Schwinger identity
$\int_0^\infty t^{-2} e^{-m^2 t - r^2/(4t)}\,dt = (4m/r)\,K_1(mr)$ proved via the substitution
$t = (r/2m)e^u$.
