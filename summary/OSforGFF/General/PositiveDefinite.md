**Informal**: A function $\varphi : \alpha \to \mathbb{C}$ is positive definite if for every $m \in \mathbb{N}$, every tuple of points $x_1, \dots, x_m \in \alpha$, and every tuple of complex numbers $c_1, \dots, c_m$, the real part of $\sum_{i,j} \bar{c}_i c_j \varphi(x_i - x_j)$ is nonnegative.
