## Overview

This file proves that the Gaussian radial basis function (RBF) kernel
$h \mapsto e^{-\frac{1}{2}\lVert h \rVert^2}$ is a positive definite function on any
real inner product space $H$. The proof proceeds in three steps: (1) the inner product kernel
$\langle x, y \rangle$ is positive definite; (2) exponentiation preserves positive
definiteness via the Hadamard series machinery from `HadamardExp.lean`; (3) the Gaussian kernel
factors as $e^{-\frac{1}{2}\lVert x - y \rVert^2} = e^{-\frac{1}{2}\lVert x \rVert^2}\, e^{-\frac{1}{2}\lVert y \rVert^2}\, e^{\langle x, y \rangle}$,
reducing the RBF to the inner product kernel.

```lean
def IsPositiveDefiniteKernel {α : Type*} (K : α → α → ℂ) : Prop
```
**Informal**: A kernel $K : \alpha \times \alpha \to \mathbb{C}$ is positive definite if for every $m \in \mathbb{N}$, every tuple of points $x_1, \dots, x_m \in \alpha$, and every tuple of complex coefficients $c_1, \dots, c_m$, one has $\mathrm{Re}\bigl(\sum_{i,j} \bar{c}_i\, c_j\, K(x_i, x_j)\bigr) \ge 0$.
