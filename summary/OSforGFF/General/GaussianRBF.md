## Overview

This file proves that the Gaussian radial basis function (RBF) kernel
$h \mapsto e^{-\frac{1}{2}\lVert h \rVert^2}$ is a positive definite function on any
real inner product space $H$. The proof uses three steps: (1) the inner product kernel
$\langle x, y \rangle$ is positive definite; (2) exponentiation preserves positive
definiteness (using the Hadamard series machinery from `HadamardExp.lean`); and (3) the
Gaussian kernel factors as $e^{-\frac{1}{2}\lVert x - y \rVert^2} = e^{-\frac{1}{2}\lVert x \rVert^2} e^{-\frac{1}{2}\lVert y \rVert^2} e^{\langle x, y \rangle}$,
allowing the reduction from the RBF to the inner product kernel.
