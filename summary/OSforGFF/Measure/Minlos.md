## Symmetry Transfer from Characteristic Functional to Measure

### [`gaussian_measure_symmetry`](../../OSforGFF/Measure/Minlos.lean#L250) вАФ Theorem

**Statement**: Let $\mu$ be a Gaussian probability measure on $\mathrm{WeakDual}\ \mathbb{R}\ E$
with characteristic functional $\Phi = \exp(-\tfrac{1}{2}C(\cdot,\cdot))$. If a continuous linear
map $g : E \to E$ preserves the covariance diagonal ($C(gf, gf) = C(f,f)$ for all $f$),
and if $\mu_{\mathrm{push}}$ is any probability measure whose characteristic functional equals the
$g$-pullback of $\mu$'s, then $\mu_{\mathrm{push}} = \mu$.
