## Minlos Theorem Applications

### [`minlos_gaussian_construction`](../../OSforGFF/Measure/Minlos.lean#L161) — Theorem

**Statement**: Under the hypotheses that $E$ is a separable nuclear space, $C(f,f) = \lVert Tf \rVert^2$
for a linear map $T : E \to H$, $C(-f,-f) = C(f,f)$, $C(0,0) = 0$, and $f \mapsto C(f,f)$ is

$$\exp\!\bigl(-\tfrac{1}{2} C(f,f)\bigr) = \int_{\omega} e^{i\langle f,\omega\rangle}\, d\mu(\omega)$$
for all $f \in E$.
