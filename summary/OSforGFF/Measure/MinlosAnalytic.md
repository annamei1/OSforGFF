## Overview

This file provides analytic infrastructure for Gaussian measures constructed via the Minlos
theorem. It introduces `CovarianceForm`, a bundled structure encoding a real symmetric
positive-semidefinite bilinear form together with the positive definiteness of the associated
Gaussian characteristic functional. The main results are: (1) invariance of the Gaussian
measure under the global sign flip $\omega \mapsto -\omega$, proved by showing the
pushforward measure has the same characteristic functional and applying Minlos uniqueness;
and (2) that the first moment $\int \omega(a)\, d\mu$ vanishes, deduced from the
sign-flip symmetry. No axioms are introduced in this file.
