## Overview

Defines time translation operators $T_s$ on spacetime points, Schwartz test functions
(real and complex), and tempered distributions, forming a one-parameter group of
automorphisms. The main technical result is a locally-uniform Lipschitz seminorm bound
$$\lVert T_h f - f \rVert_{k,n} \leq \lvert h\rvert \cdot (1+\lvert h\rvert)^k \cdot 2^k \cdot (\lVert f\rVert_{k,n+1} + \lVert f\rVert_{0,n+1} + 1),$$
proved via the Mean Value Theorem, Peetre's inequality, and the chain rule for
iterated Fréchet derivatives. This implies that $s \mapsto T_s f$ is continuous in
the Schwartz topology, as required by the OS4 (Ergodicity) axiom.
