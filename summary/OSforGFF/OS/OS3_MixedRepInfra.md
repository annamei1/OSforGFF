## Overview

Provides the integrability and Fubini infrastructure for the reflection positivity proof via
the Schwinger (heat-kernel) parametrization. The naive four-momentum integral
$\int dk_0/(k_0^2 + \omega^2)$ does not converge absolutely in 4D, so the proof goes via
the proper-time representation
$$\langle \Theta f, Cf \rangle = \int_0^\infty e^{-sm^2}\!\int_x\!\int_y f^*(x)\,f(y)\,H(s,\lVert\Theta x - y\rVert)\,dx\,dy\,ds,$$

three key Fubini theorems justifying the integration order exchanges between proper time $s$,
spatial momentum $\bar{k}$, and spacetime points $x,y$, using the linear vanishing of
$f$ at $t = 0$ and Schwartz decay for integrability. The `sorry` appearing in a comment

### [`fubini_ksp_xy_full_integrand_integrable`](../../OSforGFF/OS/OS3_MixedRepInfra.lean#L3412) — Lemma

**Statement**: For fixed $s > 0$, the full three-way integrand $(x,y,\bar{k}) \mapsto f^*(x) f(y) \sqrt{\pi/s}\, e^{-(x_0+y_0)^2/(4s)} e^{-s\lVert\bar{k}\rVert^2} e^{-i\bar{k}\cdot(\bar{x}-\bar{y})}$ is integrable over $\mathbb{R}^4 \times \mathbb{R}^4 \times \mathbb{R}^3$.

### [`fubini_ksp_xy_swap`](../../OSforGFF/OS/OS3_MixedRepInfra.lean#L3519) — Theorem

**Statement**: For fixed $s > 0$, the order of integration over $(x,y)$ and $\bar{k}$ may be exchanged:
$$\int_x \int_y \int_{\bar{k}} \bigl(f^*(x) f(y) \cdots \bigr) = \int_{\bar{k}} \int_x \int_y \bigl(f^*(x) f(y) \cdots\bigr).$$

**Proof uses**: [`fubini_ksp_xy_full_integrand_integrable`](../../OSforGFF/OS/OS3_MixedRepInfra.lean#L3412),
`MeasureTheory.integral_integral_swap`
