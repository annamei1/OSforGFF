## Overview

Proves the GFF measure $\mu_{\mathrm{GFF}}(m)$ is strictly non-degenerate, closing the
loophole that the OS axioms could be trivially satisfied by the Dirac measure $\delta_0$.
The key results are: the square-root propagator embedding $T : \mathcal{S}(\mathbb{R}^4) \to L^2$
is injective, the smeared covariance $C(f,f) > 0$ for all $f \neq 0$, every field pairing
$\langle\omega, f\rangle$ has strictly positive variance, and the pointwise kernel
$C(x,y) \to +\infty$ as $x \to y$ (UV divergence).

### [`freeCovariance_tendsto_atTop`](../../OSforGFF/OS/NonTrivial.lean#L318) — Theorem

**Statement**: $C(x_0, x) \to +\infty$ as $x \to x_0$ through $\{x_0\}^c$.

**Proof uses**: $C(x_0,x) = \frac{m}{4\pi^2 r} K_1(mr)$ where $r = \|x_0 - x\|$. Both $r^{-1} \to \infty$ and $K_1(mr) \to \infty$ as $r \to 0^+$, so the product diverges.
